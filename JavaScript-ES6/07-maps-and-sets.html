Maps & Sets

Maps and Sets are a new type of "Collections"

Maps

  • Maps are collections of Objects key/value pairs.
  • WeakMaps are not "enumerable" thus are not "iterable" either.

Sets

  • Sets are similar to Arrays but they include unique values.
  • Since Sets oly include values, key/value pairs are the same.
  • WeakSets only accept objects.
